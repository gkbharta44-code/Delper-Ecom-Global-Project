08 — Risk Analysis

Critical Risks · Mitigation Strategies · Residual Risk Ratings

R1
ETH Pool Capacity
$244.8B/mo ETH purchase required at Y0 = 60%+ of ETH market cap. Operationally impossible to absorb.
Mitigation
Regulated Multi-Asset Reserve: 40% USDC/USDT · 25% US T-Bills · 15% ETH · 10% BTC · 10% Gold-backed tokens. Reduces ETH purchases to 2.2% of market cap.
LOW after mitigation
R2
Securities Classification
DPR meets all 4 Howey Test criteria in the US. SEC enforcement action could halt operations.
Mitigation
Reg D 506(c) private placement M1–3. UAE + Singapore primary domicile. MiCA ART authorisation. Former SEC officials on Regulatory Advisory Board.
MEDIUM — jurisdiction-dependent
R3
SC Adoption Rate
70% supply chain adoption by Y5 is historically aggressive. No prior protocol has achieved this penetration.
Mitigation
Phased milestone targets (2%→8%→20%→45%→70%). Anchor partner incentive programme. SAP Ariba + Coupa integrations cover 10M+ enterprise suppliers.
MEDIUM — milestone-gated
R4
Regulatory Change
Crypto regulatory landscape shifts post-2024 elections across US, EU, India. MiCA implementation risk.
Mitigation
Multi-jurisdiction diversification. No single-market dependency. Former FCA + VARA advisors. Islamic Sukuk as non-dilutive fallback capital from Month 15.
LOW — diversified structure
